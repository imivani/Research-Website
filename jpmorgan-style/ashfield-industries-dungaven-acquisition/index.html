Ashfield Industries, Dungaven Acquisition Case

ICBC Finance Practice
Constraints - 3 Hour Time Limit, 10 Slides
Sep 2025

This analysis examined whether acquiring Dungaven Lumber created value for Ashfield Industries amid a late stage lumber cycle and rising leverage. The work combined cycle timing, cash flow durability, and debt capacity to assess whether the acquisition improved or weakened shareholder outcomes. Rather than treating the transaction as a pure growth opportunity, the analysis stressed downside protection, integration risk, and capital structure resilience. The recommendation supported a disciplined, staged acquisition approach that balanced strategic fit with balance sheet preservation in a highly cyclical industry.
