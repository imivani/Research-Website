Key read

With the share price declining and an activist shareholder involved, the core challenge was restoring credibility with the market while preserving strategic flexibility. The work focused on capital allocation discipline, portfolio complexity, and signaling effects rather than pure valuation. By separating actions that stabilize the stock in the short term from longer-term strategic options, the recommendation emphasized visible shareholder actions first, followed by measured repositioning once confidence was restored.
