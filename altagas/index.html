AltaGas Inc. Equity Research Pitch

CFA Research Challenge
Team Members: Joshua Hickling, Ali Radwan, Soumik Sarkar, Kyle Hoover Reoyo
Dec 2024
Context CFA Research Challenge
Date Dec 2024
Gallery 53 visuals
Output Case deck
Key read

The objective was to develop an institutional-quality investment recommendation supported by detailed financial modeling, peer valuation, and strategic analysis.

This report was produced in late 2024 as part of the CFA Research Challenge, where our team conducted full fundamental equity research on AltaGas (TSX: ALA). The objective was to develop an institutional-quality investment recommendation supported by detailed financial modeling, peer valuation, and strategic analysis.

The analysis highlighted de-risked cash flows supported by long-term take-or-pay contracts, a strong West Coast LPG export advantage with faster access to Asian markets, and valuation support relative to both midstream and regulated utility peers.
